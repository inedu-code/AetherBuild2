Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of construction veterans and AI experts, AetherBuild was born from a shared vision to revolutionize the construction industry through technology. We saw firsthand the inefficiencies, safety risks, and cost overruns plaguing construction projects worldwide.

What began as a small startup developing drone-based inspection software has grown into a comprehensive construction intelligence platform trusted by leading firms across the globe. Today, we combine cutting-edge AI, IoT, and robotics to deliver smarter, safer, and more efficient construction solutions.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to eliminate construction waste, reduce accidents, and bring the power of predictive analytics to every jobsite. By harnessing real-time data and artificial intelligence, we empower construction teams to work smarter, not harder.

We believe the future of construction is digital, connected, and intelligent. Our platform bridges the gap between traditional construction methods and the digital transformation sweeping other industries.
